Native Breeds: These pigs can be found throughout the country. They are a small body size compared to other exotic and crosses pig types. Their names vary from region to region, for example: Hurra, and Dharane Kalo Banggur.

Exotic breeds: The most common pig breeds for commercial pig production are the Yorkshire, Landrace, Hampshire, Duroc, and Tamworth breeds. These breeds produce lean meat combined with efficient feed conversion Compared to local pig breeds, these commercial breeds are less resistant to diseases and suffer more from hot weather and low quality feeds.
